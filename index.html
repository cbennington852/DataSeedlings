Our Mission

Data science can seem daunting. Our mission at DataSeedlings is to break down the barriers to entry by providing a hands-on, interactive, and friendly environment for learning. We believe that everyone, regardless of their background, should have the opportunity to explore the world of data.

Our tools are designed to teach foundational concepts through practical application, making complex ideas simple and accessible. We focus on building confidence and fostering a love for discovery in data. Users can learn data science through easy drag and drop objects rather than code.

Key Features

Interactive Notebooks

Learn through doing with our custom, interactive data science notebooks that guide you step-by-step.

Drag and Drop Blocks

Learn data science the intuitive way through drag and drop components.

Open source

As an entirely open-source project, DataSeedlings is built by the community, for the community.
